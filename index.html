About Us

The Art of
Oriental Dining

At Wasabee, we honor the centuries-old traditions of Japanese, Korean, Thai & Chinese cuisine while crafting something entirely new. Every dish is a canvas: from hand-rolled sushi to steaming bowls of ramen, each plate tells a story of precision, passion, and the freshest ingredients.

With two locations in Kolkata, at Bypass and Deshapriya Park, we bring authentic Oriental flavors to your neighborhood. Our chefs blend techniques from across Asia with bold, contemporary flair.
