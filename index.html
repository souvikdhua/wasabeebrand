Hon'ble Guest

Welcome to
Wasabee

We are honored and humbled by your glorious presence today and take this opportunity to thank you on behalf of every member of the Wasabee family and assure you of our best service as always.

For sheer academic reason we take pride in being the city's first standalone oriental restaurant to serve authentic Japanese cuisine alongside Burmese, Chinese, Singaporean, Malaysian, Korean, Filipino, Indonesian and Thai.

Our Chef's repertoire comprises of a wide range of oriental delicacies and to make our savory servings as authentic as possible, we use imported raw materials and ingredients. However, should you prefer, we can customize our offerings befitting our Indian taste buds based on your preference.

Please note that we only use imported salmon in our sushi and sashimi preparations.

"Bon Appetit!!!!"

Yours Truly,

TANMOY BOSE & DEBRAJ CHOUDHURY
